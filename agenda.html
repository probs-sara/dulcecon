Weekend Agenda

DulceCon is thrilled to present webinar tutorials and content on a variety of topics that will help bakeries all over the valley elevate their baking and their businesses.

Saturday, September 24


10 AM

Opening Ceremony with Jennifer Dreyer & Patrick Gisellu

Welcome DulceCon attendees! Join us for our opening ceremony with Chef Jennifer Dreyer, local celebrity chef, and Patrick Gisellu, bakery connosieur.

11 AM

Going Bananas for Banana Bread

Tres Osos Bakery brings their award-winning banana bread recipe to DulceCon! Follow along as they explain their recipe creation process and how they have stayed on top of trends.

2 PM

Bread 101 with Gawain Santiago

There are countless ways to make bread. Join Gawain from Conchita’s Bakery as he explains how Conchita’s has revolutionized their bread and what bakeries can do to elevate their own breads.

4 PM

Bringing Big Business Ideas to Life with Jenny Marisco

Learn about the logistical details and other efforts that go into visualizing a brilliant idea and bringing it to life. Join Jenny from Maria’s Bakery in this interactive presentation!

Sunday, September 25


11 AM

Baking with Brianna: Brownie Bonanza

Brianna from Tres Osos Bakery is here to explore all the ways you can make brownies! From the classic original fudge to adventurous rocky road brownies, there’s something for every bakery here.

1 PM

Baking for Good: Partnerships with Non-profits

Conchita’s Bakery is celebrating their 50th anniversary by partnering with Bakers Against Hunger, and they are sharing their process with DulceCon for bakeries who want to make a difference!

3 PM

Baguettes & Cookie Dough with Yelena Bishop

Yelena brings her twelve years of baking experience from Maria’s Bakery to this fun webinar where bakeries can learn how to make authentic French baguettes and quirky Iranian cookie dough.

5 PM

Closing Ceremony with Michael Hale & Erika Ruark

All fun must come to an end! Our closing ceremony will feature giveaways, secret product reveals, and Chef Michael Hale, local celebrity chef, and Erika Ruark, restauranteur.
